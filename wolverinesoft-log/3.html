Week 3: Wizard Attack, Beginnings of a Main Theme

Wizard Attack

I was assigned the wizard cast and hit sound effects. Unfortunately I didn't (and still don't) know what the wizard looks like in-game so I sort of went in blind. I did a bit of research on magical sound effect design, primarily by watching YouTube videos. This one was by far the most helpful. I then decided on making the wizard's magic fire-themed, so I gathered a bunch of sound effects from freesound.org including a sparkler, a wet towel on a hot plate, a large house fire, a small campfire, and some flaming stick whooshes. After a few hours of fiddly editing, I managed to turn this into what you hear below.

It seems likely that this will be too bassy or dramatic within the context of the game, but I don't know yet until it's implemented. If that turns out to be the case, maybe I can reuse these effects for the archmage champion character, who is more powerful.

Main Theme

This is where I spent most of my time; probably the better part of 2 days but I'll conservatively estimate 10 hours. I wasn't even officially assigned this, but I did it because I wanted to have a main theme in the style of Wargroove's main theme. Wargroove's main theme starts out with an independent main theme and then incorporates themes from every faction. I wanted to do the exact same thing. What I have so far is an independent main theme, and a transition section to the theme that will belong to the Asian-themed faction. For this I'm planning to use a koto and whistle to help give it a distinct sound. These two instruments can already be heard in the below file. The other two factions I'll need themes are are a Mediterranean-themed faction and a European-themed faction. I'm thinking of using an oud/bouzouki and tablas for the Mediterranean faction, if I can find samples for that, and probably switch key to something like this. For the European-themed faction I think just putting brass instruments front and center should be plenty.

My only concerns are that maybe the repetition at 0:20 isn't necessary or that the transition at 0:40 is not smooth enough.
